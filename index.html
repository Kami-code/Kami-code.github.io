Intro

I am a first-year master student in the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University, working with Prof. Abhinav Gupta on Continual Learning. I am also working as an intern supervised by Prof. Xiaolong Wang at the University of California San Diego. Previously, I worked with Prof. Cewu Lu and Dr. Wenqiang Xu in MVIG Lab, Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

My research interests include Robotics and Computer Vision, especially object manipulation, representation learning, and reinforcement learning.
