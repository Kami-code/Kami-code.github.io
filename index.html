Intro

I am an undergraduate student in Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I am supposed to get my bachelor degree in Software Engineering in June, 2023. I am currently working as an intern supervised by Prof. Xiaolong Wang at the University of California San Diego. I am also working with Prof. Cewu Lu, in MVIG Lab, Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

My research interest includes robotics, especially in object manipulation, representation learning through interaction and imitation learning.
